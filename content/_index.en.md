## About {#about}

I am a PhD candidate in Robotics at the College of Control Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University, supervised by Professors Yue Wang, Haojian Lu, and Rong Xiong. My research focuses on continuum robot modelling, kinematics, external force estimation, path tracking, and control.
